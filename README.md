# FNP-Sales-Analysis-Excel-Dashboard
Interactive Excel dashboard analyzing 1,000 FNP orders. Includes data cleaning (Power Query), modelling (Power Pivot), and dynamic visualizations (PivotTables, charts, slicers, timelines) to uncover seasonal demand patterns, sale trends . Insights highlight opportunities to optimize sales strategy and achieve a 10–15% potential revenue uplift.


## 📌 Problem Statement
Ferns & Petals (FNP) specializes in gifts for occasions like Diwali, Raksha Bandhan, Holi, Valentine’s Day, Birthdays, and Anniversaries.  
This project analyzes 1,000 customer orders (2023) to uncover sales trends, customer behavior, and product performance.

## ⚙️ Tools & Techniques
- Power Query – Data cleaning & transformation  
- Power Pivot – Data modelling & relationships  
- PivotTables – Business analysis (10+ key questions)  
- PivotCharts, Slicers, Timelines – Interactive dashboard design  

## 📊 Key Insights
- Total Revenue: ₹35.2 lakh; Avg. customer spend: ₹3,520  
- Delivery time: Avg. 5.5 days → scope for improvement  
- Seasonal peaks: Holi & Raksha Bandhan drove highest sales  
- Top categories: Colors & Cakes contributed ~45% of revenue  
- Top cities: Imphal, Dhanbad, Kavali led in orders  
- Potential revenue uplift: 10–15% via category focus & city targeting  

## 💡 Business Impact
This dashboard demonstrates how data analysis can solve real business problems by identifying demand patterns, optimizing product focus, and improving delivery efficiency.
